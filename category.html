AI DIAGNOSIS

AI diagnosis is increasingly being used in healthcare, where it can analyze large amounts of medical data like imaging scans, patient history, and lab results to assist doctors in making faster and more accurate diagnoses, particularly for identifying early signs of diseases like cancer, by detecting patterns that might be missed by the human eye; essentially acting as a powerful tool to support clinical decision-making, but not replacing the need for qualified medical professionals.

ROBOTICS IN MEDICAL FIELDS

Medical field, robotics is used to assist with surgeries, streamline hospital logistics by delivering supplies and disinfecting areas, enhance patient care by providing companionship or therapy, and generally free up healthcare professionals to focus more directly on patient needs by performing repetitive tasks like medication dispensing or data analysis
